Explore her main works

"As Meninas" (The Girls): The novel portrays the friendship among three university students during the Brazilian military dictatorship, exploring themes of oppression, freedom, and female identity.

"Ciranda de Pedra" (Circle of Stone): The book tells the story of Virgínia, a young woman who becomes entangled in a web of family secrets after her mother's death.

"Verão no Aquário" (Summer in the Aquarium):In this work, we follow the life of Mariana, a woman who, after an accident, finds herself confined to a wheelchair, facing loneliness and a transformation of her identity.

"Coração de Vidro" (Glass Heart) The author presents a collection of short stories that explore different aspects of the human condition, revealing complex characters and their experiences of love, pain, and transformation.
